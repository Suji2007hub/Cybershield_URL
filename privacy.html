Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

CyberShield is designed with privacy at its core. We do not store personal information. When you scan a URL, we only transmit the URL to the Google Safe Browsing API to determine its safety status.

2. How We Use Data

The URLs provided are used exclusively for security analysis. We do not maintain a permanent log of your scans or share your scan history with third parties, other than the security providers required for the analysis.

3. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with the Google Safe Browsing API. Your use of CyberShield is also subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

We use local storage only to remember your theme preference (Light/Dark mode). No tracking cookies are used.
